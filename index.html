Tarija, Bolivia

Tarija is a state in the suthern Bolivia. It is bordered by Paraguay to the east, Chuquisaca to the north, Santa Cruz to the northeast, Potosi to the west and Argentina to the south. The state ia a center of the wine production, paleontology parks and a mayor tourist destination for city tours and outdoor recreations.
